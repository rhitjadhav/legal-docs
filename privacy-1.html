Privacy Policy for Daily Sales Reporter

Last updated: July 4, 2025

Thank you for using Daily Sales Reporter ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and store it, and your rights concerning your information.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII):
    • Full Name: We collect the name you provide during registration (LoginActivity.java). This is used to identify you within the app and on reports.
    • Phone Number: We collect your 10-digit mobile number for user authentication via Firebase Phone Authentication (LoginActivity.java). This ensures your account is secure and uniquely identifiable.
  • Operational & Business Data:
    • Petrol Pump Name: Owners provide their pump's name, which is displayed in the app and on all generated reports (ProfileActivity.java).
    • Sales Data: All data you enter to create reports, including opening/closing nozzle readings, fuel rates, cash denominations, online payment totals (PhonePe, Card, Pinelap), credit sales details, and expense details (MainReportActivity.java, DieselReportActivity.java).
    • Generated Reports: The PDF reports created by the app, which contain a summary of all the data listed above, are stored securely.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the App's functionality:

  • To create, manage, and secure your user account.
  • To allow Owners to invite and manage their DSMs (Daily Sales Men).
  • To generate detailed daily sales reports in PDF format.
  • To store and display historical report data for viewing and analysis.
  • To pre-fill nozzle opening readings to streamline the report creation process.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is of utmost importance to us.

  • All your app data (user profiles, sales data, report information) is stored securely on Google's Cloud Firestore.
  • All generated PDF report files are stored in Google's Firebase Storage.

We rely on Google's robust security measures to protect your data. Access to your data is restricted by security rules that only allow authorized users to view or manage it.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services from Google to function:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage

These services are governed by Google's own Privacy Policy.

5. User Rights and Data Deletion

  • Owners have the right to view all data associated with their pump, manage their DSMs, and delete reports (ViewReportsActivity.java, DsmManagerActivity.java).
  • You can edit your profile information at any time within the app's "Profile Settings" (ProfileActivity.java).
  • Owners can remove DSMs. When a DSM is removed, their profile is deleted from our active database (DsmAdapter.java).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support.dailysalesreporter@gmail.com
